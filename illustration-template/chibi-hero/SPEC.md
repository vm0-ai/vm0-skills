# Chibi Hero — locked spec

A chibi mobile-RPG mascot illustration style: oversized head on a tiny body, warm-dark hand-drawn outlines, cel-shaded flat color fills, a single character caught mid-action on a transparent background. Reads as a pocket adventurer ready to drop into a card, an avatar, or an empty-state slot.

## Locked frame (never change across pieces)

- **Canvas** — 1024×1024 square, transparent PNG.
- **Proportions** — Chibi / super-deformed. Head ≈ 1× body height; head dominates the silhouette. Tiny torso, short legs, oversized hands.
- **Line treatment** — Bold confident outline in **warm dark brown** (deep cocoa, NOT pure black). Even weight, no tapered calligraphy. Outline hugs every silhouette and major form break.
- **Fills** — Cel-shaded flat color, exactly **one** tier of subtle darker shadow per surface. No gradients, no soft airbrush, no rim lights. A single flat highlight on hair or metal is allowed but stays as a flat shape.
- **Background** — Single character isolated on transparent. No scene, no ground line, no environment. Optional tiny ellipse ground shadow under feet.
- **Pose** — Always mid-action and dynamic: running, lunging, casting, sneaking, leaping, swinging, mid-stumble. Never static.
- **Face** — Simplified and expressive. Slit / narrow / determined eyes by default. Tiny mouth or none. No fine facial detail.
- **View** — 3/4 angle, camera at eye level. Never pure profile or pure front.

## Dials (vary per piece)

1. **Archetype** — ninja, mage, knight, ranger, alchemist, bard, cleric, rogue, monk, summoner, beastmaster, paladin, druid, necromancer.
2. **Palette** — outfit color family. Anchors: warm earthy, cool arcane, forest scout, steel + crimson, mustard + teal + copper, devout gold + ivory + cyan, desert ochre, ice wraith.
3. **Hero prop** — spear, longbow, staff, dual daggers, tome, hammer, sword + kite shield, smoking flask, lute, scythe, mace, war horn, summoning crystal.
4. **Pose energy** — sprint, lunge, mid-cast, low sneak, leap, ready stance, mid-stumble, swing, raised prayer, drawn-bow hold.
5. **Outfit complexity** —
   - **L1** — simple tunic / hood / pants. One layer, no accessories.
   - **L2** — layered cloak or armor + belt + 1–2 accessory pieces.
   - **L3** — full kit: pauldrons / pouches / sashes / scabbard / trinkets / goggles / cape. Busy but readable.
6. **Mood** — determined, fierce, stoic, focused, mischievous, gleeful, weary, smug, serene-focused.
